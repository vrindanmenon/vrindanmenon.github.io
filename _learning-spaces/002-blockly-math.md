Blockly Math is a small experimental space
for exploring computational thinking through
patterns, visual logic, and mathematical play.

Instead of beginning with syntax,
it begins with relationships,
instructions, structures,
and the small patterns hidden inside systems.

The intention is not just to learn programming,
but to slowly develop a way of thinking —
breaking problems apart,
observing patterns,
and learning how simple rules
can create surprisingly complex behaviour.

This space is still evolving through
experimentation, observation,
and continuous refinement.

[Explore Blockly Math](https://blockly-math-lab.vercel.app/)
