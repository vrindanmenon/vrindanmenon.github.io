# Algebra Lab

Before algebra becomes symbols on paper, it can become shape, movement, and space.

This learning space invites learners to:
- build expressions
- explore zero pairs
- arrange area models
- test substitutions
- notice patterns visually

Rather than beginning with rules, the space encourages experimentation first.

## What Can You Explore?

### Build Expressions
Drag tiles into the workspace and watch expressions form dynamically.

### Explore Like Terms
Discover which tiles combine naturally and which remain separate.

### Discover Zero Pairs
Place positive and negative tiles together and watch them cancel.

### Experiment with Area Models
Arrange tiles into rectangles and notice emerging patterns.

### Test Variable Values
Substitute values for x and y and observe how expressions change.
