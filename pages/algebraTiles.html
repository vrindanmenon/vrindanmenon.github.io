Algebra Tiles Lab

A manipulative space for exploring algebraic expressions through area, shape, and arrangement.

How to use

1

Click a tile in the tray to place it on the board.

2

Drag tiles on the board to arrange them. They snap to the grid.

3

Double-click a board tile to remove it.

4

The expression bar updates live as you add or remove tiles.
