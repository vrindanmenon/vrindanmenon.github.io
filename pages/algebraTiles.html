Algebra Tiles Lab

An experimental playground for seeing algebra through area, structure, and transformation.

How to use

1

Click a tile in the tray to add it to the workspace.

2

Drag tiles to explore different arrangements. Tiles snap to the grid.

3

Tap a tile to select it. A rotate button appears for rotatable tiles.

4

Double-click a tile to remove it.
